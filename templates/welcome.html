What is BudgetBuddy?

Many young adults struggle with managing their finances now more than ever. BudgetBuddy is a new way to reinvent banking for young adults. Our services provide categorical organization for your financial needs. BudgetBuddy allows users to create reasonable goals for their current budget and learn how to manage their money better.
Dont be shy! Give it a try!

The Future Of Budget Buddy

Integration With Users' Bank Accounts

Less work coming soon! The future of BudgetBuddy is connecting to your bank account so you don’t have to add any of your card purchases. Suggested tagging will make the process smoother and simpler! Have your account statement and your budget in the same app and let BudgetBuddy help you along the way! 

Additional Suggestions/Resources

Not only will BudgetBuddy help you keep track of your finances, but it will suggest what you should do with them too. If BudgetBuddy sees that you are spending large amounts of money on things that may not be necessary, it will show you how keeping your money can add up in the long term. Every dollar counts!
